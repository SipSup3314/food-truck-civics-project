The Whole Sha-bang

Operating out of a few trucks, we are dedicated to bringing quality Lebanese food to the average American. Our food is healthy, as well as being tasty and affordable.

Our signature dish is Chicken Shawarma, a pita bread warp consisting of chicken, tomatoes, pickles, cucumbers, and tahini sauce. All of our menu items are amazingly delicious and can provide breakfast, lunch, dinner, and dessert.

We offer authentic Lebanese food at an affordable price, and you can always expect amazing quality coming from our trucks.
